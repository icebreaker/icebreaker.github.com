Hiatus

written on August 14, 2011

For the outsider it might appear that I kind of abandoned or rather neglected this blog, but this is far from reality.

I did a bunch of awesome stuff in the past couple of months and certainly have a bunch of interesting topics that I'm going tackle during the next few weeks in order to get back on track and clear my records.

Olde New

Also, as you may noticed (if you are not reading this from an RSS reader), I switched over to a more compact, minimalistic and content centric layout.

This also means that I'll be able to focus on what matters the most the content.

I got rid of the archive and tag pages as they had low traffic which just didn't justify having them at all. Who the hell is going to browse your posts by tags nowadays?

The comments are also gone (bye, bye little Troll creatures) so you'll be forced to think twice before your dirty little fingers hit the keyboard.

Much nicer and richer syntax highlighting:

def HelloWorld():
    print('Hello World')

class HelloWorld
{
    public:
        HelloWorld(void) {}
}

Or just pre:

$ whoami
