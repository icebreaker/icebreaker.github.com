Olde New
--------
Also, as you may noticed (if you are not reading this from an RSS reader), I
switched over to a more compact, minimalistic and content centric layout. 

This also means that I'll be able to focus on what matters the most `the content`.

I got rid of the `archive` and `tag` pages as they had low traffic which just didn't
justify having them at all. Who the hell is going to browse your posts by tags nowadays?

The comments are also gone (bye, bye little Troll creatures) so you'll be forced
to think twice before your dirty little fingers hit the keyboard.

Much nicer and richer syntax highlighting:

