A Poem ... just for me ...

written on April 29, 2007

Here is a poem written by one of my friends (lol ... Gem) for Mickey (Yay, that's me!!!) ...

 

tell, tell, tell, tell

 

tell, tell, tell, tell

 

i shall tell him- who he is? i shall tell him of the barriers to be climbed?

 

A sunny day- go out and cherish one, or many (i love a mouse, she says but i'm not minnie).

 

shall i tell him- the truth of it? the eternal secret?

 

what is real?

 

to tell some things would be folly, the mouse learns but trial and error.

 

 

for mickey... hehe

Thankx Gem ... :D
